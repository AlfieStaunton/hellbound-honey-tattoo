Get To Know Us!

Alfie Santagato

Hi, I'm the owner of Hellbound Honey Tattoo — a tattoo artist with a love for bold contrast, textured realism, and unique, expressive forms. I specialise in custom artwork that blends curiosity, emotion, and storytelling into every piece.

Beyond the artwork itself, I believe the tattoo experience should feel collaborative and personal. Every session is a conversation — about ideas, symbols, aesthetics, or sometimes just life. I take the time to understand the meaning behind each design, whether it’s deeply emotional or simply something that sparks joy. No concept is too strange, too soft, or too intense; if it matters to you, it matters to me.

I approach each tattoo as a living piece of art that should flow with the body. I love exploring unusual compositions, dynamic shapes, and textures that make the tattoo feel like it belongs naturally to the skin rather than sitting on top of it. The process is slow, intentional, and detail-focused, ensuring that every line, patch of texture, or contrast plays its part in the final result.

At Hellbound Honey Tattoo, comfort is just as important as creativity. Whether you're a first-timer feeling nervous or a seasoned collector ready for your next big piece, I strive to make the studio a place where you can relax, express yourself freely, and feel truly heard. It’s more than a business — it’s a community built on trust, expression, and a shared love of art.

The Studio

Hellbound Honey Tattoo is a dark, warm, atmospheric studio home to a team of passionate artists. We focus on professionalism, creativity, and comfort for every client who walks through the door.

The studio blends alternative aesthetics with a welcoming vibe — a place where art, individuality, and expression thrive.

Whether you're coming in for your first tattoo or adding to a growing collection, we make sure your experience is unrushed and intentional. Every appointment is approached with care — from discussing your ideas to refining your design and creating a space where you can relax while the artwork comes to life. We believe tattoos are more than visuals; they're stories, transformations, and moments you carry forward.
