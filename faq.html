Pain tolerance is highly individual. The pain is often described as a stinging or scratching sensation that can vary depending on the body part being tattooed.

Follow your artist's aftercare instructions precisely. This usually involves gently cleaning the area and applying a thin layer of a recommended ointment or lotion.

Avoid swimming, hot tubs, and sun tanning until the tattoo is fully healed. Wait to go swimming until the skin is completely healed, which can take several weeks.
